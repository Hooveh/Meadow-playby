Meadow

"Hey, everything's gonna be okay. Could you tell me what's bothering you?"
-----
To humans, Meadow is a very typical fae. She's lighthearted, likes to invent little fibs, and navigates the air with such effortless, acrobatic grace that one would swear she's some sort of dancer or gymnast.

But to Meadow, she's grown so human-like, she's not sure how to feel about it--she has learned ambition, a trait few fae have, or are meant to have.

Perhaps this is by design. To live as long as fae, but with the lofty aims of humans, should seem to be unfair, even dangerous. Having centuries to enact every bit of selfishness that a human might? It could spell catastrophe.

Well, luckily, Meadow loves people. Faithful to Athelios and a devoted member of the church, she gives away much of what she owns. Material selfishness doesn't interest her, and she wishes to serve the good folks of Aphros for every century she yet has.

Whether this really solves the problem, though... is unclear.
