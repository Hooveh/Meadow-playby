Meadow

Your typical display of Fae whimsy, young Meadow comes up to a mere four inches in height, her wide blue eyes and delicate frame truly exemplary of a juvenile fairy. With a tendency to flutter incessently close on her moth wings, she embodies both curiosity and carelessness, but unlike most of her kind she finds the company of humans and humanoids reassuring and entertaining--something she may well grow out of as she ages, but for now she is content to meet new folk and revel in the differences, dangerous though it may be. Besides her strange wings, she is quite like a young human girl, with long black hair, large and watery eyes, and a slim, straight frame, albeit with long pointed ears. It goes without saying that 'Meadow' is not her True Name.
